Eggs Benedict with passion fruit hollandaise
Vegetarian
  • Difficulty: Average
  • Prep time: 30 min
  • Cook time: 5 min
  • Serving: 4 people
  • Note: The passion fruit is in season twice a year, from February to June and from late May to December. However, its puree (seeded or not) can be found frozen all year around. A little bit goes a long way. Start with the suggested quantity, and maybe you can also whip up a quick passion fruit Mimosa to go with this recipe. Cheers to that!
